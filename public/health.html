Sundaram Arulrhaj Hospitals, Tuticorin, India:

SAH is a 100 bedded multispeciality hospital in Tuticorin with all modern health care facilities and departments under one roof. We have imbibed a spirit of providing best care to the people in the Pearl City, Tuticorin.


The infrastructure and facilities available in our hospital itself portrays our commitment to give the very best to our patients and clients and their relatives, complemented by our team members, comprising of experienced full time doctors and a very efficient and cordial paramedical staff who are always on their toes to provide the best services.


SAH is the first ISO 9001 certified Hospital in the Tuticorin for its quality health care. Our Hospital has significantly and satisfactorily satisfied their latest demand of availability of the world class hospital. Our health care will be of a high order with great personal attention and involvement. We are a Public Private Limited Company ensured with the best equipments and the best care.


Backround….

Dr.S.Arulrhaj The Chairman, a renouned Cardiologist and Intensive Care Physician of India, with his aspiration of serving the needy community in the field of Education, Health care both in Preventive & Curative aspects started Sundaram Hospital in a small level with 30 bedded nursing home in the heart of the “Pearl City” Tuticorin in 1977. Due to impact of our intervention, the tireless work and dedication in his profession, Today it has grown in to a 100 bedded Multispeciality hospital in the name of Sundaram ArulRhaj Hospitals. Now his wards have stepped into the shoes of his Father, serving the needy.


We realized the need of a hospital with modern health care facilities and departments with good doctors and cordial and efficient paramedical staff functioning under one roof. In a very short time, we have significantly enhanced our reach and have established ourselves as the important choice for those who were looking for high powered hospital equipped with able doctors.

Rural Medical Centers


Health for all by 2000 was our India’s slogan. To contribute for the same, Our foundation has started adopting the Health of a village at Ramdass Nagar, under the name “Family Clinic”. Every Sunday one Medical Officer is conducting OPD Services & Medicines are given free.


The following facilities are also offered:

  • Free sugar checkup and drugs
  • Free BP Checkup & drugs
  • Free Special Medical OPD Services of SAH are also offered to them.

Rural Health Sub Centres:

Sundaram ArulRhaj Hospital has established sub-centres at villages in Tuticorin District with the assistance of Sterlite. A Medical Officer and an attendant staff are there in sub centre. “Thinking globally and acting locally” is the byword followed in this Subcentre. These sub centres are at the reach of the poor and provide the villages with primary and secondary medical care. A characteristic feature of these sub centres is Community Participation and Contribution in Health Care to serve about 50,000 population and make them accessible health services. Free Health Awareness Camp:


SAH constantly works towards dispelling fears and misconception related to the most prevailing diseases like Heart diseases, Neuro problems, Diabetics, Cancer, HIV/AIDS in the rural areas of Tuticorin Districts. It also concentrates on increasing the health awareness level and treatment seeking behaviour among the rural population through various camps for improving their health status. Co-ordinating with Voluntary Agencies. Check up, investigations & drugs are given freely ..


i) Diabetes awareness camp.

Diabetic Exhibition and explanation to patients about Diabetes, Dietary advice & Treatment options. Public Questions & Answers sessions is also conducted. Urine sugar & Blood sugar are checked and drugs given freely.

ii) Heart Care Camp & Heart Surgeries selection.

BP. ECG are checked freely. Selected cases undergo Echocardiogram & eligible cases are chosen for surgery.

iii) General Medical Camp:

Team of Doctors visit the village and offer Health checkup and distribute free drugs
