Chairman Message

Warm seasons & health greeting from Dr.S.Arulrhaj Health & Educational Foundation Tuticorin. Being located in the Southern most corner of India, a Family of doctors have committed to offer affordable & accessible health care to the community. And also after educational activities to the marginalized children in the villages & health professionals. With this objective the above charitable Organization, was founded in 1995.


Health & Education are the two eyes of human life. Doctor & Teacher are respected in the society for their selfless services to create a Healthy & Strong Society leading to a HEALTHY INDIA.


This Charitable Institution is offering secondary level health care, nearly four decades at Tuticorin through Sundaram Arulrhaj Hospitals, a Hundred bedded, Multispecialty medical Institution.


Having understood the need for extension of their services to the unreached, the following services are offered through this Trust.


On the EDUCATIONAL FRONT We are conducting paramedical courses, in nursing, Lab Technology, Physiotherapy to the poor children of the villages mostly free. Getting support from many Government & Non Governmental Agencies.


In addition, Post graduate medical courses are offered to Doctors in the spear of Internal Medicine, Family Medicine & Emergency medicine.


Our Trust through this hospital offers Training platform for paramedics & medics.


On the HEALTH CARE FRONT, our aim is “HEALTHCARE TO THE UNREACHED“. Our Rural extension services, emergency medical care in the golden hour of Trauma and emergency, Accident a relief centers located on national highways, Health Messages & services are spread in the culturally sensitive manner through health awareness programmes through Tv, video, camps & Pattimantram. Our recent activities against controlling harmful use of alcohol & non communicable diseases like Diabetes, is well recognized by the people.


Our special school for the “differently abled” is unique for this Pearl city.


We want to expand our Health & Education services to the people & ultimately reach our vision “HEALTH UNIVERSITY BY 2016”


On behalf of our team I invite Support & Participation by like minded well-wishers, on Technical, Physical & Financial Strategies.


ENVISAGING A “HEALTHY VIBRANT INDIA”.


Prof.Dr.S.Arulrhaj MD., FRCP (Glasg)
Chairman & managing Trustee
